QROYAL MOBILE APP - ROLE-BASED DESCRIPTION

=========================================
SECTION 1: CUSTOMER INTERFACE (MOBILE WEB)
=========================================

1. Landing & Authentication
- Public entry point for users to understand the loyalty system.
- Direct CTA to "Get Your Card" (Signup).
- "Scan to Login" button to quickly access profile via existing QR codes.

2. Signup & Onboarding
- Minimalist form capturing Name and Phone Number.
- Immediate generation of the "Universal QR Code" digital identity.

3. Home Dashboard (The Digital Wallet)
- Personalized greeting and current date.
- Real-time progress tracker for the most active loyalty program (points/stamps).
- Floating Action Button (FAB) to instantly display the personal QR code for store scanning.

4. Discover & Search
- Tool to find participating businesses.
- Categorized lists for "Popular", "Nearby" (GPS-based), and "All".
- Instant "Join" buttons for one-tap enrollment in new programs.

5. Personal Profile
- High-visibility display of the personal QR code with a Print option.
- Profile picture upload and personal info management.
- Account deletion and support contact options.

6. Business Profile (Customer View)
- The landing page for a specific shop.
- 3-Tab View: 
    - [Posts] for news and videos.
    - [Discounts] for active coupons.
    - [About] for contact info, map, and social links.

=========================================
SECTION 2: BUSINESS INTERFACE (KIOSK MODE)
=========================================

1. Kiosk Scanner
- Full-screen, high-performance camera interface.
- Designed for tablets or store-front smartphones.
- Continuous scanning capability for rapid customer processing.

2. Scan Confirmation Overlay
- Real-time feedback window after a scan.
- Displays Customer Name, points awarded, and updated total.
- High-visibility success/reward alerts for store staff.

3. Walk-in Provisioning
- "Create QR" button to generate temporary IDs for customers who don't have the app yet.
