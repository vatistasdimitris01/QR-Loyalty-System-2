QROYAL PC/DESKTOP APP - ROLE-BASED DESCRIPTION

=========================================
SECTION 1: BUSINESS OWNER PORTAL
=========================================

1. Dashboard & Analytics
- The core management hub.
- 7-Day Performance Charts: New Members, Points Awarded, Rewards Claimed.
- Real-time "Points per Scan" and "Reward Threshold" editor for immediate program adjustments.
- Widget Customization: Toggle visibility and drag-to-reorder analytics cards.

2. Customer Management (CRM)
- Searchable database of all loyal customers.
- Individual member cards with point totals and contact history.
- Ability to manually award/deduct points or remove members.

3. Content & Promotion Studio
- Post Manager: Rich text (Markdown) editor to publish news and announcements.
- Discount Manager: Creation tool for digital vouchers and time-limited offers.
- Multimedia support: Image uploads and YouTube video embedding.

4. Profile & Branding Center
- Public Profile Editor: Upload logo, cover photo, and business bio.
- Social Link Integrations: Facebook, Instagram, and Website URLs.
- Location Settings: Geocoding tool to set physical store location for the mobile map.

5. QR Design Lab
- Business QR Stylizer: Customize the look of the business login code.
- Customer QR Library: Create unique branded styles that customers can apply to their own digital cards.

=========================================
SECTION 2: SUPER ADMIN (PLATFORM) PORTAL
=========================================

1. Platform Oversite
- Master view of every registered business on the network.
- Global customer list for cross-platform support.

2. Global Management
- Post Moderation: View and manage all announcements across the platform.
- Membership Logs: Track every point transaction and membership joining event globally.

3. Partner Provisioning
- Professional onboarding tool to create and configure new business partner accounts.
- Direct database management for troubleshooting and data maintenance.
